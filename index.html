Soil Qualities and Recommendations

Improving soil quality not only boosts agricultural yields, but also improves soil fertility, reduces erosion, and increases soil's capacity to store carbon.

Analytics

pH Value

Indicates the degree of its acidity or basicity, ranging from 0 to 14, where 7 represents neutrality. This value has a significant impact on the nutrient availability for plant growth and the well-being of soil microorganisms.

NPK (Nitrogen, Phosphorus, and Potassium) Content

The NPK content of soil reflects the quantity of these nutrients present. Soil testing on a regular basis can assist guarantee adequate NPK contents in the soil for healthy plant development.

Humidity Level

The humidity level affects plant growth, as well as the activity of soil microorganisms. Optimal soil humidity is important for healthy plant growth, as well as for maintaining a balanced soil ecosystem.

Temperature Level

It affects various processes in the soil, such as seed germination, nutrient uptake, and microbial activity. Optimal soil temperature is important for healthy plant growth and a balanced soil ecosystem.

Plant and Crop Recommendations

These systems take into account the specific needs of the plants in an area and make recommendations about the best ways to improve the soil and the recommended plant and crop.
